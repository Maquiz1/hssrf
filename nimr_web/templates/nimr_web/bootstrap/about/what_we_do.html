ABOUT Health and Social Sciences Research Foundation

QUALIFICATIONS AND PAST PERFORMANCE OF THE Health and Social Sciences Research Foundation (HSSRF):

HSSRF established due registration act No. 12 of 2002, and was incorporated on 09 day of March 2016. and was given registration No. 124548, under BRELA. And was then registered under amended law order of act 2002 on 30 th August 2019 under terms and condition of Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) under Ministry of health in Tanzania. With the main objective of Conducting health and social Sciences researches as well as analyze health and social policies for the purpose of providing evidence based on sound recommendations to the policy makers and implementers. HSSRF is a not for profit making Non-Governmental Organization, non-partisan and non-religious which was established in 2016. The motive for its establishment was after realizing that there was a drift in linking health and social sciences research in lower and middle income countries. HSSRF stands a chance to leverage health and social research by providing evidence for policy formulation, implementation and analysis. This will help in bridging the gap of social determinants with biomedical model for better yield in improving wellbeing of the community.
