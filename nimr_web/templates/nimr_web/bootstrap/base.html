WORD FROM CEO

On behalf of Health and Social Sciences Research Foundation(HSSRF), I am humbled to welcome you to our website. This website serves as our quick information tool to our partners and stakeholders. We strive our best to link-up our stakeholders such as researchers, decision makers, policy makers, scholars, media personnel and the general community through a quick but yet concise presentation of what we do through this website.

 

 

This website presents our vision, mission, core values, core functions and it is perceivable as a nerve center for our growth. Being a member to regional and international Health and Social Sciences research, our website provides a wordwide exposure to enhance prominence. For the past six years, we were delighted by your support in leveraging the knowledge gap among key stakeholders in health and social sciences which are key to a healthy population. We have grown in heights because of you. We are continuing to improve our services to take in the changing dyanamics in technology and knowledge translation means.
