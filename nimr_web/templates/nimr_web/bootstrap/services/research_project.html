Research & Project Management

HSSRF conducts research and manages projects aiming at generating and advancing knowledge. We intend to provide evidence that will inform policy formulation and improve health systems. HSSRF research agenda includes health and social issues standing in the way of high quality of health and social welfare. Research with us is shaped by knowledge gaps in bio medical sciences and social determinants of health. Through partnering with renowned Research institutions, we perform high quality research, maintaining good conduct and dissemination of results to intended audience.
